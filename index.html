Yophish — Cybersecurity Awareness Training

Yophish guides security teams through every step of cybersecurity awareness training—from onboarding and navigation to campaign launches and results—all within a single, focused dashboard.

Mission

Provide organizations with a clear, repeatable playbook for phishing simulations: easy account creation, intuitive login, transparent navigation, and actionable insights that help people learn without friction.

Features

Seamless Onboarding

Clear create-account and login flows get new users into Yophish quickly.

Guided Navigation

Overview, Staff Statistics, New Campaign, Result, and Setting stay pinned on the left.

Overview Insights

Timeline graph plus six KPI circles summarize sent emails, opens, and success rates.

Staff Statistics

A dedicated table lists every employee's department, open status, and link clicks.

New Campaign Builder

Add recipients manually, pick from two templates, and enable open/click tracking.

Result & Setting

Per-campaign outcomes highlight risk levels, and Setting keeps logout one tap away.
