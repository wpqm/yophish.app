Yophish – Cybersecurity Awareness Training Platform

Yophish is a cybersecurity awareness training tool designed to help organizations safely simulate phishing attacks for educational purposes.

Our platform sends simulated phishing emails to employees to measure awareness levels and teach best practices for recognizing social engineering attempts. No real malicious activity or harmful content is involved.

Our Mission

To help companies strengthen their security posture by training their teams against phishing threats in a safe, controlled, and ethical environment.
